About Us

The Covenant Helpers Foundation is a Non-Government Organization or charity founded to cater for the poor and needy in Nigeria and Africa in general. As there exist a huge number of families and children who live less than $1 a day and we intend to fill in the gap to ensure that human basic needs, such as food, shelter and clothing are provided.
